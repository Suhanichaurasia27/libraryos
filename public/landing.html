Features

Everything You Need to Run Your Library

Built for Indian library owners. Simple, fast, and complete.

👨‍🎓

Student Management

Add, edit, and remove students. Track their seat number, phone, email, and monthly fee. Search and filter instantly.

💰

Fee Tracking

Automatically generates monthly fee records. Track paid, partial, and pending status for every student every month.

📧

Email Reminders

Send automatic reminder emails to students with pending fees. Scheduled on 25th and 28th every month.

📊

Reports & CSV

Monthly summary reports, defaulter lists, collection percentages. Export data as CSV anytime.

🔐

Secure & Isolated

Each library owner gets their own account. Your student data is completely private and isolated from others.

⚙️

Customizable Settings

Set your library name, monthly fee amount, email credentials, and reminder message template. All per library.

How It Works

Up & Running in 2 Minutes

1

Register Your Library

Enter your library name, your name, email, and password. Takes 30 seconds.

2

Add Your Students

Add each student with their seat number, phone, and email. Fee records are created automatically every month.

3

Track & Collect Payments

As students pay, record their payment. The dashboard shows your collection percentage and who still owes.

4

Send Reminders Automatically

Connect your Gmail and the app sends reminders every month automatically. No manual work needed.
